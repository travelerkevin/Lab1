Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains how this website uses cookies and how you can manage them:

1. A cookie is a small file placed on your computer that helps analyze web traffic or lets you know when you visit a site.

2. I use cookies to understand which pages are being visited and to improve user experience.

3. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

4. Disabling cookies may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

5. No personal information is stored in the cookies used on this site.
